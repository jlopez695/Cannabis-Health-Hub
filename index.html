About Cannabis Health Hub

The Cannabis Digital Health Information Portal is an online platform that guides users through cannabis-related digital technologies, with a focus on mobile apps. By cataloging and assessing these tools, it enables individuals to match apps to their needs, make informed choices, use cannabis responsibly, and advance public health. As mobile devices become a primary source of health and lifestyle guidance, the portal serves as a trusted entry point into the evolving landscape of cannabis digital resources.

Currently, the portal presents a curated set of cannabis-related apps identified through a qualitative research study. It will expand into a comprehensive hub where users can discover, evaluate, and engage with a broad range of cannabis digital tools and resources. The initiative is affiliated with the Cannabis Research Institute and the University of Illinois.

How To Use This Portal

The Cannabis Digital Health Information Portal helps users find cannabis-related mobile apps that match their needs. Apps are organized into three main categories:

Recovery

Recovery:

Apps that support individuals who are trying to pause, reduce, or stop using cannabis.

Cultivation

Cultivation:

Apps that assist users in growing cannabis or recognizing different cannabis plants and products.

Consumer

Consumer:

Apps aimed at those using cannabis for entertainment, wellness, or medical purposes.

Each category offers filters for specifics like dispensary locations, cannabis recipes, and strain databases to narrow results
